Separated from mainland by Laguna Madre through which passes the Intracoastal Waterway. Small channel at N end separating it from Mustang Isl. has been filled; N end is opposite city of Corpus Christi (W), connected to it by causeway. Separated from Brazos Isl. on S end by Brazos Santiago Pass, 8 miles N of mouth of Rio Grande (Mex. border), Brownsville Shipping Channel enters Gulf through Pass.
