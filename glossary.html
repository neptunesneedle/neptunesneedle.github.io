Glossary


Aback

Wind on the forward side of a square sail; pushing the ship backwards or at least slowing it down


Abaft

Aft of (behind) the beam (midway point) and toward the stern (back) of a vessel but not directly behind (i.e., "astern")


Abeam or A Beam Reach

Position on vessel at right angle to the vessel centerline, equidistant between the bow and stern; also, situation when wind is crossing the boat so that it passes across amidships


Able bodied seaman

Member of the crew with some prior experience at sea; with skills above those of an "ordinary seaman"
